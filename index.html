SSC - A CYCLING CLUB FOR THIRSK & SOWERBY

Join our vibrant cycling community in Thirsk and experience the joy of riding together. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or a beginner, SSC has something for everyone. With over 100 members of all ages and abilities, we offer a variety of rides and events throughout the week. Sign up today and become a part of our cycling family!

About Sowerby Sunday Club

Founded in [Year], the Sowerby Sunday Club (SSC) is a welcoming and inclusive cycling club based in Thirsk. Our mission is to promote cycling as a fun and healthy activity for people of all ages and skill levels. With a strong community spirit, we organise various rides and social events that cater to recreational cyclists and those looking to compete.

Rides & Events

SSC offers an array of club rides throughout the week. Whether you prefer road cycling or gravel paths, we have something for you. Check out our regular rides below or find the full list on our Strava page.

Thursday Club Night

01

Join us for our Thursday club night at 6:30, at the cenotaph in Sowerby. Whether you're looking for a relaxed evening ride, ideal for unwinding after a long day, or a fast-paced drop ride, we offer it all. With groups ranging in pace from 14mph up to a flat out 21-23mph.

Tuesday Club TT

02

Join us for a race of truth; test yourself against the clock over 10 miles. The best bit is coming back the week after to try and beat your time.

Midweek Rides

03

We have an active group of midweek riders who are well-seasoned cafe experts and route planners, with rides ranging from 30 miles up to 65. There is always someone to get out on a ride with.
